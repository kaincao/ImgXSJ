Google AI Studio Build Mode Review - I give it an 8/10.

- comparable to other AI app builders in quality, but free to use (they must be losing money on this, no?)
- turns simple/broad ideas into working micro apps in very short time... speed was impressive, actually
- struggled with analyzing a YouTube video link, unfortunate since it's owned by Google
- BIGGEST WIN: AI studio integrates with Google products and can build with existing data from those apps. Google Maps shown in demo below. 

It gets an 8/10 compared to other providers, largely because of the Google product integrations.

Also, the burger at Spruce is fantastic.
